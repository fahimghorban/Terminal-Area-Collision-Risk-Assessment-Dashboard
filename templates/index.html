Welcome to the Airspace Collision Risk Assessment Dashboard

This tool visualizes projected near mid-air collisions (PNMAC) at major U.S. airports. It uses a projection method called Projected Closest Point of Approach (P-CPA), which estimates how close flights will get to one another in the future â€” rather than just their current proximity.

The safety metric is called Projected Near Mid-Air Collision (PNMAC). We define three zones:

  • PNMAC-1: T2CPA < 30 sec, D@CPA < 0.25 NM, Vertical < 150 ft
  • PNMAC-2: T2CPA < 60 sec, D@CPA < 0.5 NM, Vertical < 250 ft
  • PNMAC-3: T2CPA < 90 sec, D@CPA < 1 NM, Vertical < 500 ft
